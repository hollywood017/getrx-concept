How It Works

Five steps. Under 60 seconds.

From patient selection to pharmacy delivery — the fastest compliant prescription workflow ever built.

Select Patient
Search by name or ID, pull from your existing roster or EMR. Your full patient history, instantly available.
Choose Drug & Dosage
Browse the full U.S. pharmacy formulary, including Schedule II–V controlled substances, with instant DEA verification.
Set Instructions
Frequency, quantity, refills — entered in standard medical notation. Smart suggestions speed up your workflow.
Verify Pharmacy
Auto-filled from patient history and searchable nationwide. Confirm the pharmacy in one tap — no phone calls.
Send Instantly
DEA-verified, end-to-end encrypted, timestamped delivery in seconds. Stored automatically with full audit trail.
Controlled Substances

The only prescription pad that handles it all.

For years, controlled substance prescribing meant paper pads, phone call overrides, and compliance headaches. getRx eliminates all of it — standard and controlled, one workflow, one app.

Write Schedule II–V prescriptions directly from iPhone — no paper, no phone overrides, no fax machines.
Two-factor authentication built into every controlled substance Rx for full DEA compliance.
Full audit trail automatically generated — every Rx, timestamped, identity-verified, searchable.
Delivered instantly to the pharmacy — same speed as a standard prescription.
Critical for vets writing controlled pain medications, surgeons managing post-op care, and remote physicians.
