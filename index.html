The only prescription pad that handles it all.

For years, controlled substance prescribing meant paper pads, phone call overrides, and compliance headaches. getRx eliminates all of it — standard and controlled, one workflow, one app.

  • Write Schedule II–V prescriptions directly from your iPhone — no paper, no phone overrides
  • Two-factor authentication built into every controlled substance Rx for DEA compliance

  • Critical for vets writing controlled pain medications, surgeons managing post-op care, and remote physicians
  • Delivered to the pharmacy instantly — same speed as a standard prescription
