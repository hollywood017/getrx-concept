How It Works

Three steps.
Zero friction.

getRx strips away everything that slows prescribers down. No phone calls. No hold music. No fax machines. Just prescriptions, sent.

Select Patient

Pull up any patient from your roster in seconds. Full history, allergies, and prior Rx — all loaded before you type a word.

02
